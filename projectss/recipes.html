Beef & Cabbage Stir Fry

    Ingredients
  • 8 oz ground beef
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic & ginger
    Steps
  1. Brown beef in skillet.
  2. Add garlic & ginger, cook 30s.
  3. Add cabbage & carrot, cook 5 min.
  4. Add soy sauce, mix well.

Bowties & Broccoli

    Ingredients
  • 8 oz bowtie pasta
  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper
    Steps
  1. Cook pasta, add broccoli last 3 min.
  2. Drain, toss with oil and cheese.
